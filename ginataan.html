Ginataang Langka

A delicious Filipino dish made with young jackfruit cooked in coconut milk.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg young jackfruit (langka), peeled and sliced
  • 2 cups coconut milk
  • 1 cup coconut cream
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 thumb-sized ginger, sliced thinly
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 2 pieces red chili, optional
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Sauté garlic, onion, and ginger until fragrant.
  2. Add the sliced jackfruit and cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Pour in the coconut milk and bring to a simmer. Cook for 15-20 minutes until jackfruit is tender.
  4. Add the coconut cream and red chilies (if using). Simmer for another 5-10 minutes, stirring gently.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Adjust consistency by adding a bit of water if needed.
  6. Serve hot with steamed rice.
