The Story in the Book

Chapter 1

Molly had been staring out of her window for about an hour now. On her desk, between copies of Nature, New Scientist, and other journals, was a well-thumbed copy of On The Road. It had been her favorite book since college, reminding her of freedom beyond her walls.

She had spent the last ten years in this room, sitting under a poster quoting Oscar Wilde: "Work is the refuge of people who have nothing better to do". Many praised her work unraveling llama DNA, yet Molly felt there was more to life than science alone.
